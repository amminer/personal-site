Hi,
I'm Amelia

and I'm a graduate student at Portland State University's Maseeh College of Engineering and Computer Science with previous experience in general biology. I'm interested in using computers to bridge the gaps in-between people as well as between us and the rest of the natural world. I believe software is a uniquely powerful and flexible tool for conveyance of meaning and understanding, opening the door to experiences people would otherwise be unable to have. To me, responsible and effective software engineering helps everyone to better understand one another and facilitates easier, more comfortable living for everyone.
